    2014 DOORWAYS FOR HOPE GALA

    HomeAid’s annual Doorways for Hope gala will be held on Friday, November 7, 2014 at the elegant Balboa Bay Club in Newport Beach. This year’s theme celebrates HomeAid’s 25th Anniversary with a Silver Masquerade Ball as HomeAid Orange County strives to Unmask the Faces of Homelessness.

    Please plan on attending this elegant affair and join 350-400 like-minded friends of HomeAid Orange County as we come together to celebrate the work HomeAid has done over the past 25 years and raise much-needed funds for the work yet to be done to end homelessness.

    Save the Date

    Friday, November 7, 2014

    Location

    Balboa Bay Club
    1221 West Coast Highway
    Newport Beach, CA 92664

    Sponsorship Opportunities

    The Doorways for Hope gala is the premier fundraising event for HomeAid Orange County held in the fall each year to garner support from the greater community. All funds raised go directly to support HomeAid’s shelter development, advocacy, and outreach services. Sponsor benefits vary by level, however all are recognized on the event webpage, e-marketing materials, printed materials, in signage, program book and include tickets to the event.

    Gala Sponsorship Giving Levels:

    • Hope Sponsor - $25,000
    • Dream Sponsor - $10,000
    • Angel Sponsor - $5,000
    • Inspiration Sponsor - $3,000
    • Faith Sponsor - $1,500

    *Individual Ticket - $275

    Donations to the auction are also welcome

    To learn more please contact Anita Lambert at (949) 553-9510 x225 or anita@homeaidoc.org.
